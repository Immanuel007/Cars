OUR TEAM

More than 20 years ago, this company started with three highly driven car traders. Their hard work and passion laid the foundation for this company. Years later, AA Japan is now a leading car exporter from Japan and has grown to around 200 worldwide. By simplifying the process of importing vehicles, we have reached countless customers around the world and earned their trust. A MULTICULTURAL TEAM

Just like our customers, our team is multicultural and multinational. Of course, our roots are Japanese and so are many of our team members. But because we want to understand you, your culture and your background, our team also welcomes people from around the world.
